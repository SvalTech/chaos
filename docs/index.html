How Squad Works

Multiplayer accountability.

1. 1-on-1 Connections

Squads are built through direct connections. Share your 6-digit code or link to add a friend.

2. Building a Group

There are no central "group links." To make a squad of 3 or more people, everyone must mutually add each other's codes.

3. Live Status

When you start your timer, your squad sees a pulsing red dot indicating you are currently deep in focus.

4. Task Syncing

Your "Today" tasks automatically sync with your squad so they can hold you accountable (toggle in Settings).
