Welcome to ChaosPrep.

ChaosPrep is a quiet, distraction-free tracking system designed specifically for JEE, NEET, and competitive exam aspirants. No AI fluff, no social feeds—just raw productivity tools to help you hit your targets and visualize your growth.

Initial Setup

When you first sign in, you need to configure your environment to match your goals. ChaosPrep adapts to your specific exam requirements.

  • Select your Exam: Head to Settings (the gear icon) and select JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, or Custom. This automatically configures your subjects (e.g., swapping Maths for Biology) and your default target dates.
  • Add Custom Subjects: Studying for boards or an extra subject? Use the + Custom button in the Quick Add Task menu or go to Settings to add subjects like "English" or "Physical Education".
  • Set your Target Year: Ensure your target year and session are set correctly so your Reality Check countdown is accurate.

Daily Planner

The Planner is where you convert broad syllabus goals into executable daily tasks.

Adding and Organizing Tasks

Click the + Add Task button on any day card. Select a subject, write your objective, and hit Add. To reorder tasks within a day, open the Day View by clicking on a specific day card, and drag the tasks using the grip handle on the left.

Subtasks

Tasks can be broken down further. Open the Day View, click on a task, and type into the "Add subtask..." input field to create a micro-checklist for complex chapters.

Focus Timer & Radio

Clock actual deep work hours. The Timer view prevents you from lying to yourself about how much you actually studied.

  • Subject Tracking: Select a subject before hitting play. When you stop the timer, the session is logged to that specific subject.
  • Minimum Time: Sessions under 1 minute are discarded to prevent log spam.
  • Focus Radio: Need background noise? Click the floating music icon in the bottom right corner to open the Focus Radio. It streams live lo-fi, synthwave, and classical music via YouTube without needing a premium subscription.

Analytics (Mocks & Errors)

Data drives improvement. ChaosPrep separates your analytics into Mock Test tracking and Error tracking.

Logging a Mock Test

To log a mock, add a task in the Planner and select Mock as the subject. This unlocks special score input fields. Enter your obtained and max marks. Once completed, this data feeds into the interactive chart in the Stats view.

Error Logging

Don't just look at your mock score; analyze your mistakes. In the Error Tracking view, you can log specific mistakes by Subject, Chapter, and Type:

  • Conceptual: You didn't know or misunderstood the theory.
  • Calculative: You knew the method, but messed up the math.
  • Silly Mistake: Misread the question, marked the wrong bubble, etc.
  • Formula: Forgot or misapplied a formula.

The system generates a "Mistake Profile" pie chart so you know exactly what kind of errors are costing you the most marks.

Syllabus Tracker

A comprehensive, pre-loaded list of chapters based on the latest NTA guidelines.

Instead of simple checkboxes, ChaosPrep uses a phased approach: To Do → Doing → Done → Mastered. You can also track how many times you have revised a chapter and whether you have completed the Previous Year Questions (PYQs) for it.

Squads (Multiplayer)

Studying for competitive exams can be incredibly isolating. Squads is a multiplayer accountability feature designed to simulate the feeling of studying in a library with your hardest-working friends. Surround yourself with winners.

Building Your Squad

Unlike WhatsApp or Discord, there are no centralized "Group Links." Squads are built entirely through mutual, 1-on-1 connections. This prevents massive, distracting groups from forming and keeps your circle tight.

  1. Find Your Code: Go to the Squad view. At the top, you will see your unique 6-character Invite Code (e.g., A1B2C3).
  2. Share It: Click the code to copy it, or use the "Link" button to generate a direct invite URL to send to a friend.
  3. Mutual Connection: To form a squad of 3 people (You, Alice, and Bob), everyone must mutually add each other. You must add Alice, Alice must add Bob, and Bob must add You.

If someone clicks your invite link, they will see a special "Your squad is waiting" login screen. Once they sign in, they are instantly added to your dashboard.

Live Status & The "Pulse"

The most powerful feature of Squads is live presence. We don't track your location or keystrokes—we track your focus.

Studying

When a user turns on their Timer, their status instantly turns red. The pulse indicates they are in deep focus.

Idle

The timer is off, but the user has been active in the app within the last 15 minutes. They are planning or taking a break.

Offline

The user has closed the app or hasn't interacted with it for over 15 minutes. Shows "Seen X hrs ago".

Task Syncing (Privacy)

By default, the tasks you schedule for Today are visible to your Squad. As you check them off in your planner, they will cross out on your friends' screens in real-time. This creates positive peer pressure.

If you prefer to keep your to-do list private, go to Settings → Share Tasks and toggle it off. Your friends will still see your live study status, but your tasks will be hidden.

Themes & Preferences

Make the workspace yours.

  • Dark Mode: Toggle manually in settings or let it follow your system.
  • Atmosphere: Choose a preset background (Minimal, Space, Abstract) or paste an image URL to set a custom wallpaper.
  • Accent Themes: Not a fan of purple? Switch to Matcha (Green), Cobalt (Blue), Crimson (Red), or Monochrome (Black/White).
  • Lite Mode: Running ChaosPrep on an older laptop or budget phone? Toggle Lite Mode to disable heavy glass blurs, shadows, and animations to save battery and boost speed.

Day Rollover (For Night Owls)

By default, calendar apps reset at 12:00 AM (Midnight). But if you are a student who studies until 3:00 AM, having your planner jump to "Tomorrow" in the middle of your study session is frustrating and breaks your streaks.

The Fix: Go to Settings and change your Day Rollover. If you set it to 4:00 AM, ChaosPrep will consider it "Today" until 3:59 AM. Your tasks, streaks, and charts won't reset until you actually go to sleep.
